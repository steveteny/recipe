RECIPE OF BEEF STEW

Ingredients

  • 1kg meat
  • Chopped onions
  • chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2litre of cooking oil
  • two Royco beef flavor

    • PROCEDURE


    To heated cooking oil add chopped onions, fry them until they turn golden brown,


    add chopped tomatoes and fry as you swirl until they become squachy,

    then add the sliced beef and stire until it forms a broathe.

    AFter frying the beef in the tomato and onios,one spoonful of table salt and 2beef flavor Royco cubes.

    Leave for about 20minutes after which it form a brothe and an arromatic smell,

    then take it off the jiko and serve it with Ugali
